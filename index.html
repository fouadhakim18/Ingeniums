About Us!

Our Goals

Ingeniums Club is working towards bridging the gap between each and every CS student, to support their undergraduate experience and to provide a community that students can belong to and identify with. With hundreds of CS students joining every year,Ingeniums aims to run events that encourage students to connect and socialize, to increase technical and academic interest with workshops and talks.

Our Story

We are students ourselves, coming from all computing backgrounds. Ingeniums Club was born from an unfulfilled need; we felt that although there are so many students at ESI-SBA that are interested in CS, there wasn't a proper support network in place. By recreating what Ingeniums Club means for our community, we're passionate about helping students redefine their university experience.

Our Mission

The Ingeniums Club team is devoted to providing you with all the resources you could need as a student. We want to create a community that members can call home, to make it a place where you can reach out for career advice, for academic help, or even just to socialize. To fulfil this mission, we'll be running events and initiatives throughout the term, so please check out our Events section below to stay updated!

Our Vision

  • 1.Academic: Promoting the knowledge and interest of Computer Science.
  • 2.Career: Providing career guidance and resources to help students gain experience and knowledge for their own job search.
  • 3.Community: Encouraging interpersonal relationships through community building and social events for all computing students.

Some events

Welcome Day September

"Welcome Day" is an event where you will be introduced to the club's different departments and structure, meet our members, and also hear some of their experiences, which will help you to make your decision.

Install Party October

Install Party is an event where the experienced students help you install Ubuntu or Kali on your PCs.

Amazigh Day January

To cherish and celebrate our ancestry, Amazigh day is back again with new surprises. It will take place this Tuesday 11/01 at 1pm, where we're going to celebrate YENNAYER and discover the different algerian cultures (Kabylie, Chaoui, Mzab).

Ingeneer January

Get ready to test your engineering competencies and put your skill to use with us on INGE-NEER. To shape yourself as an engineer and prepare yourself for the professional world, you will have to make your way through the hard gripping challenges with only one thing on your mind, SURVIVAL.

Esi Showcase March

ESI SHOWCASE is an open space for students who want to prepare their Academic Project where they check a variety of projects of the old students..

Art Manchour May

Art is the primary tool of communication. It allows people from different cultures and times to understand each other via images, sounds, and stories.

IngeHack June

IngeHack is a CTF competition (Capture The Flag) organized by Ingeniums which resolves in a series of challenges in the Cyber Security field that the contestants (in teams or individually) need to solve. The CTF challenges' levels varies from beginner to intermediate.

Inge Podcast All year long

Each Friday a live will be held by many people in different fields and specialities to share their experiences and academic careers so as their knowledge.
